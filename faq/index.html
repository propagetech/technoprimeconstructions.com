What kind of homes does TechnoPrime Constructions build?

We build premium residential homes in Bangalore that are amenities-rich, eco-friendly, safe and Vastu-aligned. We combine the latest building technology with time-tested engineering and the highest-quality materials, with a highly personalised approach for every client.

Where is TechnoPrime Vasudha located?

TechnoPrime Vasudha is at # 37, KSRTC Layout, 5th Cross, 3rd Main, J. P. Nagar 2nd Phase, behind Ragigudda Temple, Bangalore 560078, an established and well-connected South Bangalore neighbourhood.

What makes your homes eco-friendly?

We make creative use of the natural elements, earth, water, wind and light, to build homes that blend harmoniously with nature. Eco-friendly, amenities-rich and safe design is part of how we plan every project.

What does Vastu-aligned design mean for my home?

We incorporate Vastu elements into our buildings to give our clients spiritual and holistic satisfaction, so the home feels balanced and supportive of wellbeing, alongside modern comfort and function.

Do you guarantee on-time delivery?

Yes. Guaranteed on-time delivery is one of our core commitments, so you can plan your move with confidence. We keep planning transparent from concept to handover.
