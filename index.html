ABOUT US

Our Story

TechnoPrime Constructions, as our name suggests, is a combination of the latest technology and the time-tested engineering concepts. Our core leadership team is spearheaded by ace Engineers, Mr. Ravishankar M B and Mr. B Basavaraju with 50+ years of experience, along with the tech-savvy Mr. Ashuthosh M.R, a third generation builder.

Our core strength lies in our ability to grasp and fulfill customer aspirations through unique concepts, personalised planning, complete transparency, and, above all, structures that epitomise excellence and the highest quality materials. We use the latest building technologies that make our constructions amenities-rich, eco-friendly, safe and delightful. And to do that, we have put together a select team of engineers, consultants, and architects, with years of experience and expertise.
